Property Description:

Superior 5 bedroom detached house located on substantial grounds on Anglesea Road in Dublin 4. Available to occupy immediately, this house comes unfurnished. The 3 storey house is built mainly over ground floor level which comprises a large garage, utility room and games room. This house exudes modern contemporary living and is the perfect family home or embassy residence. The house is accessed through secure electronic gates just off Anglesea Road and along a smashing leafy driveway shared with another superior home.

The living accommodation comprises of 5 double bedrooms, 3 ensuite bathrooms and a WC. There is ample amount of storage in the basement area of the house which can be accessed through the double garage on ground level by electronic door. The ground floor utility room hosts a laundry shoot from the 2nd floor.

On the first floor you are greeted by a bright and airy entrance hallway that uses natural light throughout and this leads into the open plan kitchen and 2 large reception rooms. This floor opens out onto an expansive raised stone patio area spanning most of the rear part of the house and over looks beautiful West facing gardens.


The property is situated 5 minutes walk from Ballsbridge village where there is a host of amenities such as cafes, restaurants, the RDS. All public transport links such as bus routes and the DART are easily accessible as is Dublin City Centre. Other local amenities include Merrion Cricket Club, Old Belvedere Rugby Club, Donnybrook Village and The Aviva Stadium. The location gives access to many private and public schools both primary and secondary as well as being easily accessible to UCD and Trinity College.

There is ample parking and viewing is a must for this special house.

Viewing is by appointment only.

Rent allowance not accepted
