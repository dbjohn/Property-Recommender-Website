Property Description:

Newly renovated spacious (c. 1,000 sq ft) bright ground floor apartment in this well established and much sought after quiet development.
Prime city centre location close to shops, restaurants and all other amenities. Ten minute walk to St Stephens green.
This apartment boasts plenty of living space comprising of a separate entrance hall, 2 large double bedrooms one en-suite and a main bathroom with bath/shower, large living and dining room and a separate spacious kitchen looking out onto landscaped gardens. The living/ dining room leads through patio doors to a patio seating area set in a south facing secluded garden.

The apartment has been recently renovated throughout including new appliances and has plenty of storage comprising of two separate storage areas.

Secure car parking included.

Rent allowance not accepted
