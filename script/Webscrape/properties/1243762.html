Property Description:

No 1 Clyde Rd is a delightful, fully restored period house on a particularly quiet road opposite the American Embassy and St Bartholomew's Church. It is located behind electric gates and boasts two car parking spaces. The recent restoration was a no expense spared effort and the condition of the building can only be described as flawless. All dealings are directly with the landlord and the exterior of the property and front and rear gardens are fully maintained as part of the deal. This property has not been on the letting market since 2004 and represents an excellent chance to secure rental accommodation of the highest standard.
