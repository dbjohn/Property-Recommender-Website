Property Description:

Beautiful detached recently refurbished four bedroom family home
located in one of the finest parts of Dublin 4.
This wonderful property has undergone a complete refurbishment to
the very highest standard.
The house comprises of two double bedrooms and two single
bedrooms, with main bedroom en suite, and separate family bathroom
shared between other bedrooms. The bedrooms have excellent storage
space with tranquil fixtures and fittings.
The living room is spacious and bright, beautifully furnished
(solid wooden floors) with Gas fire place and access to wonderful
private garden with BBQ area that would be ideal for entertaining.
A study room or bedroom is located next to the living room and
looks on to the back garden area that is very quiet and perfect
for working from home.
The kitchen is the lovely stand out feature of the property with
granite worktop, gas hob, cream cabinets/charcoal grey floor tiles
and all new appliances.
There is a large storage/utility room located off the kitchen that
is truly excellent for storage with access to back garden and
front driveway.
The house has been fitted with cat 5 cabling for extra fast
internet speed/ Superior house alarm system with text message
alerts and a state of the art heating system with hot water on
demand.
Other features include car parking for four cars, small garage for
storage/ lovely landscaped greenery/ two minutes walk to
Sandymount Dart station/ beside Sandymount Village and fantastic
beech/ five minutes walk to Ballsbridge and the Aviva stadium/near
to a host of Gyms/close to IFSC and access to the Port tunnel/
beside the best restaurants and hotels in Dublin City.
Viewing by appointment. Weekends and late viewings available.
The house is heated by Gas central heating.

Rent allowance not accepted
