Property Description:

Number 32 Brook Court is a charming detached two storey house in a very pleasant position beside Monkstown Village. The excellent balance of living and bedroom space would ideally suit a family who require a safe and quiet location.
The accommodation comprises of delightful hallway which generates a welcoming atmosphere, Attractive living room(wooden floors) with plenty of natural light leading to dining room through inter connecting french style doors. The living room is fitted with a Gas fire place. The upstairs part of the house consists of three double bedrooms with the main bathroom en suite and extra large bathroom located between the other two bedrooms. There is also another small room which would be perfect as an office or baby room. All bedrooms have very good wardrobe space. There is also space for storage in the attic.
The kitchen is located at the back of the house with easy access to the garden. The kitchen is quite spacious with all appliances/tiled floor and boasts a separate large pantry. There is also a separate utility room off the kitchen with washing machine and dryer.
To the front of the house off the hallway there is another room which is currently set up as a baby room.
Another stand out feature of this property is the back garden which is well enclosed, beautifully landscaped and will receive the sun all day. There is also a garden shed located at the end of the garden.
Private driveway parking.
Gas Central heating.
