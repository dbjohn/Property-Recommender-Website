Property Description:

Spacious 1 bed/1 bathroom apartment available in luxury, modern purpose built secure development. Situated on the 3rd Floor with panoramic views of City, Dublin Bay and Wicklow mountains. It is fully furnished and it has a large double south facing balcony overlooking Custom House Square. It is just a short walk to the City Centre and is close to all amenities and public transport inc the Bus terminus and both The Luas and Dart metro/ tram lines. The Luas... stop at Custon House Sq is outside the front door. The apartment has one double bedroom and one full bathroom. It has a fully integrated kitchen with oven, hob, extractor fan, microwave, fridge and washerdryer. A parking space is also available.
